Our Services

我们的服务

Each treatment is carefully tailored to your individual constitution and health goals. No two treatments are the same.

🪡

Acupuncture

针灸

Fine needles stimulate specific meridian points to restore the flow of Qi, relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and support your body's natural healing response.

🌱

Herbal Medicine

中药调理

Customised herbal formulas prescribed to address the root cause of your condition, not just the symptoms. Available as decoctions, powders, or capsules.

💨

Cupping Therapy

拔罐疗法

A traditional technique using suction cups to improve circulation, release deep muscle tension, and clear stagnation of Qi and Blood.

🔥

Moxibustion

艾灸

Gentle heat therapy using dried mugwort to warm the meridians, strengthen Yang energy, and support immunity and digestive health.
